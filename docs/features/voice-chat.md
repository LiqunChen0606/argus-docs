# Voice Chat

Voice Chat lets you speak your questions and hear AI responses read aloud. It works in Chat mode as a hands-free way to interact with Supercharger.

## Speaking a Message

Tap the **microphone button** next to the text field in Chat mode. Supercharger uses iOS Speech Recognition to transcribe your voice into text. When you stop speaking, the transcribed message is sent automatically.

Requirements:
- Microphone permission (prompted on first use)
- Speech Recognition permission (prompted on first use)

## Voice Mode

Voice mode is a continuous hands-free experience where every AI response is automatically spoken aloud.

**To activate voice mode:**
1. Enter Chat mode.
2. Tap the **speaker icon** in the toolbar to enable voice mode.
3. A visual indicator confirms voice mode is active.

**While voice mode is active:**
- Every new AI response is read aloud using text-to-speech.
- You can still type messages normally if you prefer.
- Tap the mic button to speak your next question.

**To deactivate voice mode:**
- Tap the speaker icon again. Any in-progress speech stops immediately.

## Speaker Replay Button

Each AI response bubble has a small **speaker button**. Tap it to hear that specific response read aloud, even if voice mode is off. This is useful for replaying a long answer or listening while multitasking.

If the AI is currently speaking, tapping the button on a different message stops the current speech and starts the new one.

## Choosing a Voice

You can pick which voice Supercharger uses for text-to-speech:

1. Open **Settings**.
2. Tap **Voice**.
3. Browse the available voices for your language.
4. Tap a voice to select it. A preview plays so you can hear how it sounds.

The voice list comes from your device's installed speech voices. You can download additional voices in **iOS Settings > Accessibility > Spoken Content > Voices**.

## Tips

- Voice mode works well for quick questions while cooking, driving, or exercising.
- Long or technical responses (code, lists) are better read on screen -- tap the speaker button selectively for those.
- The speaker replay button works on any past message in the conversation, not just the most recent one.
- Voice input and voice output are independent -- you can speak questions without enabling auto-speak, or enable auto-speak while typing.

# Widgets

Argus provides four home screen widgets that give you quick access to information without opening the app.

## Available Widgets

### On This Day

Shows a memory from this date in a previous year. Displays the LLM-generated narrative (if available), the year, and the number of items from that date. Tap to open the full Time Capsule explorer.

**Best size:** Medium or Large

### Search Stats

A quick glance at your search activity. Shows your total search count and this week's count. Useful for tracking how actively you use Argus.

**Best size:** Small

### Quick Search

A tap target that opens Argus directly to the search screen. On iOS 18+, this is also available as a Control Center widget for even faster access.

**Best size:** Small

### Morning Brief

A daily summary widget powered by proactive suggestions. Shows upcoming events, trending topics from your data, and any activity bursts detected. Content refreshes each morning.

**Best size:** Medium or Large

## Adding Widgets

1. Long-press on your home screen until the icons jiggle.
2. Tap the **+** button in the top-left corner.
3. Search for "Argus" in the widget gallery.
4. Browse the available widgets and sizes.
5. Tap **Add Widget** and position it on your home screen.
6. Tap **Done** to save.

Alternatively, on iOS 18+:
1. Long-press on your home screen.
2. Tap **Customize** at the bottom.
3. Tap **Add Widget** and search for Argus.

## Widget Settings

Some widgets can be configured after adding them:

1. Long-press the widget on your home screen.
2. Tap **Edit Widget** (if available).
3. Adjust settings like preferred time range or content type.

## How Widgets Stay Updated

Widgets refresh automatically through iOS background updates. Data is shared between the main app and widgets through the App Group (`group.com.4SGM776BB8.supercharger`). The main app writes fresh data periodically, and widgets read it on their next refresh cycle.

Widget data updates happen:
- When you open the main app
- During background refresh (approximately every 30 minutes)
- When iOS schedules a widget timeline update

## Tips

- The On This Day widget is most engaging when you have several years of data indexed. Enable Photos and Calendar for the best results.
- Stack multiple Argus widgets in a Smart Stack to cycle through them automatically.
- Quick Search widget pairs well with the iOS 18 Control Center widget for app-free access.
- If a widget shows stale data, open the main app briefly to trigger a refresh.
